Use Cases: The customer will launch the ATM machine and enter in the customer number and PIN. The the system will display the account selection screen. The customer will then have then have the two options to Checking or Savings account. The system will then show the transaction entry screen which will allow the customer to enter the amount for the transaction on the keypad. Then the customer will have the two options to select deposit or withdrawal. The system will then perform the transaction, debit or credit the account as appropriate and return to the main screen.
